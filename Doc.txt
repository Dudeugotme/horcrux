
Horcrux
-------

Reducto:
	- Create Local Horcrux

	Disperso:
		- Puts them in different places (may be manual to begin with)

Revelo:
	- Client facing
	- FS Layer, local FS (FUSE)
	- Uses Accio and makes it visible to users

Accio:
	- Gets chunks for Revelo
	- Checks them for integrity
	- Puts them locally in WORKDIR

Accio Backends
	- cp (Locket)
	- scp (Nagini)
	- aws
	- azure
	- gcloud
